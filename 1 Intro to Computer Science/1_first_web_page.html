Greatest Innovation

I believe the greatest innovation in technology is the invention of open source software and services. Before open sources came along, companies like Microsoft could monopolize and overcharge for software and other services since competition was few and far between. Once other developers were given access to create and sell software, prices began to drop and more people had access to the wealth of information the internet provides today. People can also use open source platforms to share information more readily with sites like Wikipedia. This has allowed our world to be more connected than it was before, although there is now more doubt about the credibility of the information we read. Although open source sites may have their problems, I think their costs are worth the greater interconnectedness we have today.
